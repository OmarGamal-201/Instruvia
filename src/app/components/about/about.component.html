Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate educators and technologists, Instruvia emerged from a simple observation: traditional education wasn't keeping pace with the rapidly changing world.

What started as a small platform offering a handful of courses has grown into a comprehensive learning ecosystem serving thousands of students across the globe. We've helped professionals upskill, students pursue their passions, and businesses train their teams.

2020
Founded with 10 courses and 100 students
2021
Expanded to 500+ courses and launched consultation services
2022
Reached 10,000+ learners and introduced mobile app
2023
Global expansion with 50,000+ learners worldwide
